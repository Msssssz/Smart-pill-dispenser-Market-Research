Reimbursement Pathway

Reimbursement Pathway — US vs Key Markets
The US is the only market with confirmed, quantified billing codes that let institutional operators recover device adoption cost through insurance. RTM codes 98980/98981 cover therapeutic monitoring management. CCM codes 99490/99487 cover chronic care management — requirements include an electronic care plan, 20 min/month documented care coordination, clinical supervision, and patient consent. Whether medication adherence data from a dispenser supports these requirements is an open question.

Who is already competing and where is the open gap?

The Case for US B2B Institutional — Supply-Side Evidence

The institutional dispensing model is proven at scale globally. North America has no equivalent — only hardware players, no platform with EHR integration. The capability maps below show every hardware player worldwide and US-only: EHR integration is absent across all 19 US players, present only in Evondos globally.

Player Distribution by Continent — B2B Competition Established, EHR Gap Confirmed
Europe has exactly one full-stack B2B institutional platform: Evondos (HW + Platform + Service + EHR-equivalent workflow integration), headquartered in Finland and expanding across EU. North America has 5 B2B institutional hardware players but zero with a platform layer. The commercial model is proven at scale — the North American equivalent does not yet exist.
